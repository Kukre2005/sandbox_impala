Things to know about Cabo and frequently asked Questions

About Cabo area

How far away is San Jose del Cabo from Cabo San Lucas?

Cabo San Lucas is the largest town in the municipality of Los Cabos. San Jose del Cabo is the next largest town in Los Cabos. Los Cabos also includes tiny towns, the coast, the hills, & the mountains, all of it.

The towns of Cabo San Lucas and San Jose del Cabo are about 33 km (20 miles) apart. The Transpeninsular Highway or Highway 1; is marked in kilometers from downtown Cabo at km 0.

How far away is SJD Airport from San Jose del Cabo?

SJD Airport is located in San Jose del Cabo, but the resorts and tourist scene part of town is located at 12 km (8 miles) from the airport, driving through town on highway 1, it is about 25-35 minutes depending on traffic; there is a toll road from the airport going directly to San Jose del Cabo tourist corridor, taking this option, the drive takes 15 minutes approximately; The rate for the PRIVATE airport transfer includes the cost of the toll road!

SJD Airport is located in San Jose del Cabo, Cabo San Lucas Downtown is located at 50 km (80 miles) from the airport, driving through San Jose del Cabo on highway 1, it is about 50-60 minutes depending on traffic; there is a toll road from the airport going directly to San Jose del Cabo tourist corridor, taking this option, the drive takes 35 minutes approximately; The rate for the PRIVATE airport transfer includes the cost of the toll road!

Transportation is very different depending on the Los Cabos area that your hotel is located; If you went for the convenience of staying in Cabo San Lucas downtown area, you might find must of the attractions in town to be walking distance from your hotel, places like Puerto Paraiso mall, Medano Beach and a lot of very popular restaurants are located in an area not bigger than 10 blocks square.
If your hotel happens to be anywhere in the corridor area, your options for going to either San Jose or Cabo San Lucas are public transportation (Ruta del Desierto bus), the local taxi’s or if you are a “plan in advance” type of traveler, you can always check out our private transfer options with one of our agents, they will make recommendations on restaurants and things to do, as well as help you pre-arrange your transportation needs, for more information call +1-877.401.1789 or go to the live chat in this page!
If you decided on the beautiful and calmer town of San Jose del Cabo, you will enjoy the walks through its little main plaza and the souvenir shopping, main square it’s only about a mile away from most of the resorts, but if you need to go to any Restaurant or activity on the tourist corridor or even Cabo San Lucas, your best option is to make arrangements in advance, having your private chauffer picking you up at your convenience and not stressing about finding a Taxi after your activities; Feel free to check our recommendations in the “Dinning” and “Activities” section, feel free to call one of our agents, they will be more than glad to help you plan your perfect vacations!

If I choose the Cabo Shuttle option, how long will I wait at the airport for my Car to leave?

Shuttle service was designed for travelers who want to experience the best service at the lowest cost by sharing the transportation with other travelers arriving at similar time than you, by similar time, we mean not particularly on the same flight, this means we have to wait for other travelers to go through customs and come meet us outside, this can be anywhere from 20 to 40 minutes, there is not an exact time frame.

Can I get on a shuttle once I get to the airport, without previous reservation?

Unfortunately, Los Cabos has some restrictions when it comes to last minute services due to Taxi union regulations, if you get to SJD airport without a previous reservation, we are forced to deny you the service, your only option is to take a local taxi.
