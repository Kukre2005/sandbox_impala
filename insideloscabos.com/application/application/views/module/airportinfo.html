Airport Information

Los Cabos International Airport

Welcome to one of the busiest airports in Mexico! SJD (according to the IATA code) is located in San Jose del Cabo and serves not only San Jose and San Lucas, but also the whole Los Cabos area (one of the 5 municipalities in the Baja Sur).

Besides Cabo San Lucas’ private airport, SJD is the only airport in the area, and one of the 3 international airports in the Baja Sur. La Paz International Airport is 75 miles away, and Loreto International Airport is 221 miles from San Jose del Cabo.

Terminals

This airport used to have three terminals with four concourses but as of now, only two terminals are in function, making traffic more fluid. Terminal 1 is used for domestic flights, while Terminal 2 replaced terminals 3 and 4 as the one for international flights. It has four jet bridges and six non jet bridge gates.

Interesting facts about Los Cabos Airport

This is the 6 th busiest airport in Mexico.3,130,986 passengers were greeted during 2014, and 3,523,010 during 2015. The architect in charge of the 1997 renovation was Manuel De Santiago-de Borbón González Bravo; he is the great-grandson of Queen Isabella II of Spain. Most people fly into Cabo from Los Angeles CA, Dallas TX and Phoenix AZ. The rest of the top 10 cities is formed by San Diego CA, San Francisco CA, Houston TX, Denver CO, Orange County CA, Calgary Canada and Chicago IL.
