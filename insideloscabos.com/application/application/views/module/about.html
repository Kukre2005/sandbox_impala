About Los Cabos

Amazing coastal scenery, dramatic cactus-dotted desert landscapes and miles of white sandy beaches. Los Cabos is the place where you can truly relax and enjoy life, now wonder it is prefered by thousands of American Visitors and residents. It`s geographical separation from mainland Mexico, has given this area a distinct "north-of-the-border" atmosphere and attitude. The more secluded town of San Jose del Cabo reflects this life-style, while retaining a serene atmosphere, Shopping in Caboperfect for relaxing on the beach and romantic dinners by candlelight.

Its sister city, Cabo San Lucas, is a bustling center of activity, and is considered by many visitors to be the center of the Los Cabos resort scene. Along with its sparkling nightlife, gourmet restaurants, and superb shopping, the busy marina offers delightful experiences such as an afternoon Sunset Cruise or a stroll to the sport fishing dock to view the day's catch. The area offers endless opportunities for sightseeing in town, out in the country or out on the water.

Fishing, Golf, Snorkeling and More...

Los Cabos crystalline waters are teeming with sea life, which makes this place unique and an ideal spot for snorkeling and diving. This place is renowned for the best game fishing in the world, diplaying over 800 species of game fish. Novices and experienced fishermen can be sure to experience the thrill of hooking a Marlin, sailfish or dorado, to name just a few.

If adventure is the name of the game for you, escape to the rugged outback on an ATV and take a drive through the desert. You may also enjoy horseback ride on the beach at sunset. Find more information on "Things to Do".

Shopping and Nightlife

When you get tired, there is always a sunset stroll along one of the many beaches. Lover's Beach is a favorite, where the waters of the Sea of Cortez and the Pacific meet in an array of blue nuances. What an amazing time to feel the breeze in your hair and listen to sound of the waves mixed with the distant cries coming from the birds colonies nesting on the off-shore rocks! As Los Cabos has grown, so have the opportunities for shopping. Visitors will find many souvenir shops selling all types of Mexican handicrafts, silver, and ceramics.

Ah, at last! the freedom you've been dreaming of is here for you to enjoy. Nightlife in Los Cabos is primarily centered in San Lucas. Many bars and restaurants close at 10 or 11 p.m., but your nightlife choices can continue until the wee hours for those looking to party till morning.
