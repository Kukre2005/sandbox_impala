Fishing

Cabo San Lucas is known around the world for it's sportfishing. The waters of the Baja are full of world-class billfish and prized catches like Tuna, Snapper, Marlin, Mahi Mahi, Wahoo, and more. MARINA AT PUERTO LOS CABOS

The Marina at Puerto Los Cabos is the gateway to the Sea of Cortez and the world’s fines sportfishing grounds. Just ten miles from the renowned Gordo Banks, Puerto Los Cabos fishing departures put you closer to the fish that "didn't get away" providing you with the most memorable deep-sea, big game fishing experiences of a lifetime and making it dollar for dollar the best alternative for fishing in Los Cabos.

With slips that hold boats of up to 400 feet in length the Marina at Puerto Los Cabos provides the most exclusive, all-inclusive, secure, fully enjoyable and relaxing destination experience that you will find anywhere in Mexico. A “4 S” Marina, that provides security, service, shelter and serenity in pristinely beautiful natural surroundings in a friendly, service oriented tranquil atmosphere. Currently the Marina at Puerto Los Cabos have 200 slips available and a boatyard with Drystack and a 150-ton travel lift in place.

MARINA AT CABO SAN LUCAS

The IGY's Cabo San Lucas marina features 380 slips and accommodates vessels up to 375' (114m) with a max 26' (7m) draft and boasts the highest quality services and amenities expected at a luxury yachting marina. Strategically located in the harbor of Cabo San Lucas, at the very tip of the Baja Peninsula, the marina hosts renowned sport fishing tournaments for marlin, tuna, dorado, and wahoo. With many offerings located on-site and just steps away including diverse charters, restaurants, entertainment and shopping venues, Marina Cabo San Lucas is clearly one of the globe’s foremost sport fishing destinations.
